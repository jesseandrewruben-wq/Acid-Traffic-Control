ACID TRAFFIC CONTROL is a 16-step groovebox combining a synthesizer sequencer and a drum machine — displayed through a live radar scope interface.
◆ SYNTH — a 16-step acid bass sequencer with filter, LFO, and delay. Aircraft on the scope represent active notes.
◆ DRUMS — kick, hi-hat, and clap rows beneath the synth grid. Ground structures on the radar mark active drum hits.
◆ CONTROLS — adjust BPM, swing (Formation), density, turbulence, filter and delay from the lower panel.
